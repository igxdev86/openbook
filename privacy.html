Privacy

What we collect

An email address (to run your account and send match notifications), your bids and matches, an optional display name, and basic technical logs needed to keep the service secure. That's the lot — we don't ask for your card, address or identity documents, because we never take payment.

How we use it

To operate the order book, notify you when a retailer accepts your bid, maintain fair-use measures (such as completion history), and improve the service. We do not sell your data, run advertising trackers, or share your details with retailers beyond what a match requires — a retailer never sees who is bidding, only the anonymous demand.

Where it lives

Account data is stored with Supabase on infrastructure in the UK/EU. Sign-in is passwordless via email links, and we use only the cookies strictly necessary to keep you signed in.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you can request a copy of your data, correction, or deletion at any time — email support@openbook.co.uk. Deleting your account removes your bids from the book and your personal data from our systems, except records we must keep for legal reasons.

Who is responsible

The data controller is IGX Ltd (England and Wales). Questions or complaints: support@openbook.co.uk. You also have the right to complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.
