What is OpenBook?

The shop where you set the price

OpenBook is an open marketplace for phones that works like a stock exchange. Instead of paying the shelf price, you place a order — the price you'd genuinely pay today for the exact phone you want, brand new and sealed. Your order joins a public order book alongside everyone else's.

Retailers accept orders in bulk

Verified UK retailers watch the demand on their stock. When the numbers work for them, they accept orders — often hundreds at once. If yours is accepted, you're matched instantly at your exact price, never more.

You check out with the retailer, not with us

A match gives you a 30-minute checkout link to the retailer's own store at your matched price. Payment, delivery and warranty all come direct from the retailer. OpenBook never takes your money and never touches the goods — we simply run the market.

Free for buyers

Ordering is free, requires no card, and you can cancel a live order any time from your account. You're only ever committed at the moment a retailer accepts your price — and even then, all you've committed to is the chance to buy at the price you yourself named.

Why it works

Every phone on OpenBook shows the cheapest current retail price alongside the live orders. Retailers use OpenBook to move stock efficiently without slashing prices in public; buyers use it to pay less than the shops without haggling, auctions or waiting games. The spread between the two is where deals happen.
