Support

How do I place a order?

Open any phone's market page, enter your price and tap Place order. You'll sign in with a one-tap email link the first time — no passwords. One live order per phone, cancellable any time from your account.

What happens when a retailer accepts?

You'll get an email straight away and the match appears in your account with a 30-minute countdown and a checkout link to the retailer's store at your exact matched price. Complete the purchase within the window and the phone is yours.

What if I don't complete in time?

The match simply expires. Nothing is charged — you never gave us a card. Repeatedly letting matches expire will stop your future orders counting towards the visible demand, so only order prices you'd genuinely pay.

Who am I actually buying from?

Always a verified UK retail business, never OpenBook and never a private individual. Payment, delivery, returns and warranty are all with the retailer under their normal terms and your normal consumer rights.

Is every phone new?

Yes — every market on OpenBook is for new, sealed, UK stock unless a market page clearly states otherwise.

Something else?

Email support@openbook.co.uk and we'll come back to you as quickly as we can.
